Information about the overworld

Overworld

The Overworld is divided into biomes of various types. A biome's type determines the basic characteristics of the terrain within it, such as the blocks composing the surface or the plants that thrive in it. The biome type also determines which mobs can spawn within it and affects how environmental behaviors such as weather are expressed within it.

Unique features of the Overworld include

Sleeping in a bed skips the night and sets spawn point (explodes when used in other dimensions).
The most biomes of any dimension.
Surface and underground biomes.
Oceans containing water.
The most amount of ores.
Many naturally generated structures.
The most types of mobs.
A daylight cycle.
Weather.
A total vertical height of 384 blocks, as opposed to 256 blocks for other dimensions.
